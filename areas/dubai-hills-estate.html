Why Dubai Hills Estate

Dubai Hills Estate is Emaar Properties' most ambitious masterplan community to date, developed in joint venture with Meraas across over 2,700 hectares in the geographic centre of Dubai. The community is anchored by an 18-hole championship golf course designed to USGA standards, setting the tone for a neighbourhood defined by open space, considered landscaping, and architectural cohesion. From landmark villa enclaves to mid-rise apartment clusters, every sub-community within the masterplan follows a unified design language that appeals strongly to end-users and institutional buyers alike. The scale and quality of delivery have made Dubai Hills Estate a benchmark against which other Emaar launches are measured.

Residents benefit from one of the most complete lifestyle ecosystems in New Dubai — Dubai Hills Mall anchors retail and dining within the community, while Dubai Hills Park provides 180,000 sqm of green space with cycling tracks, jogging paths, a skate park, and a dedicated dog park. Connectivity is exceptional: Al Khail Road (E44) and Mohammed Bin Zayed Road (E311) place Downtown Dubai roughly 20 minutes away and Business Bay within 15 minutes, making the commute viable for professionals working across the city. The tenant profile skews strongly toward families and senior corporate executives, with GEMS Wellington Academy, GEMS International School, and GEMS New Millennium School providing world-class education from within or adjacent to the community. King's College Hospital and Genesis Healthcare Centre complete the on-site amenity stack.

From an investment perspective, Dubai Hills Estate offers the rare combination of a Tier-1 developer covenant, genuine lifestyle scarcity, and an undersupplied rental market relative to demand. Buy-and-hold strategies work exceptionally well here — both villa and apartment sub-markets have posted consistent annual price growth since 2020, and the completion of the Dubai Metro Blue Line extension earmarked for the area is expected to further compress yields in the medium term. Investors targeting generational assets or family-office allocations in real estate will find few comparable propositions in Dubai at this price point.

Investment Data

Investment Highlights

5.5 – 6.5%

Average Rental Yield

Apartment units in Park Heights and similar mid-rise clusters consistently achieve the upper end of this range, driven by strong corporate and family demand. Villa yields sit at the lower bound but benefit disproportionately from capital appreciation, making total return the correct metric for that asset class.

AED 1,500 – 2,500

Average Price per sqft

Entry-level apartments in established clusters trade at around AED 1,500/sqft while premium villa plots and golf-facing units command AED 2,200–2,500/sqft. The spread reflects genuine scarcity at the top end and creates a tiered entry opportunity for investors at different capital thresholds.

20 – 35%

Capital Appreciation (3yr)

Dubai Hills Estate has been one of the top-performing masterplan communities over the last three years, with golf-facing villas and limited-supply townhouse enclaves outpacing the city average. Sustained demand from end-users, combined with limited plot releases, underpins this trajectory into the medium term.

84 – 90%

Occupancy Rate

Occupancy across the community sits well above the Dubai residential average, reflecting the quality of lifestyle infrastructure and the depth of the tenant pool. Shorter void periods and stable lease renewal rates make cash-flow modelling more predictable here than in comparable suburban addresses.

Off-Plan Opportunities

Key Projects

Golf Place

Developer: Emaar Properties

Golf Place is Emaar's signature villa address within Dubai Hills Estate, positioned directly on the golf course with expansive fairway views and resort-grade landscaping. Units range from four- to six-bedroom standalone villas and offer some of the largest plot sizes available within the masterplan. The combination of scarcity, direct golf frontage, and Emaar's delivery track record makes Golf Place one of the most sought-after addresses in New Dubai for serious capital allocators.

Park Heights 2

Developer: Emaar Properties

Park Heights 2 is a mid-rise residential cluster fronting Dubai Hills Park, offering one- to three-bedroom apartments with direct park access and competitive entry pricing within the masterplan. The project targets the rental market sweet spot — professionally designed, well-connected units that appeal to young families and dual-income professionals. Payment plans and phased handover schedules make Park Heights 2 an accessible entry point for investors deploying AED 1.5–3M.

Ellington House

Developer: Ellington Properties

Ellington House represents the boutique, design-led alternative within Dubai Hills Estate, developed by Ellington Properties whose reputation for interior finish quality commands a premium in the resale and rental markets. The project features one- to three-bedroom apartments with bespoke amenity programming — rooftop pool, landscaped podium, and curated resident experiences — that differentiates it from the broader Emaar supply. Investors seeking stronger yield premiums from tenants who prioritise quality over price will find Ellington House a compelling allocation.

Lifestyle and Amenities

Transport

Dubai Hills Estate is served by Al Khail Road (E44) and Mohammed Bin Zayed Road (E311), providing direct access to Downtown Dubai in approximately 20 minutes and Business Bay in 15 minutes. The planned Dubai Metro Blue Line extension will bring a station to the community, a catalyst that is already being priced into forward valuations. Multiple bus routes and ride-hailing infrastructure serve day-to-day mobility needs within the masterplan.

Shopping and Dining

Dubai Hills Mall — one of the largest malls in the emirate — anchors the community's retail and dining scene with over 650 stores, a cinema complex, an indoor skydiving facility, and an extensive food and beverage offering spanning casual to premium dining. A dedicated community retail strip along Dubai Hills Boulevard provides everyday convenience options within walking distance of most residential clusters.

Parks and Recreation

Dubai Hills Park spans 180,000 sqm and is one of the largest parks in New Dubai, featuring dedicated cycling and jogging tracks, a skate park, a children's adventure play area, and a dog-friendly zone. The 18-hole championship golf course is open to residents and members, with a full clubhouse offering pro-shop, dining, and event facilities. Extensive internal cycling and pedestrian networks connect sub-communities throughout the masterplan.

Education and Healthcare

GEMS Wellington Academy, GEMS International School, and GEMS New Millennium School are all located within or directly adjacent to the community, offering British and IB curricula from Foundation Stage through A-Level. King's College Hospital Dubai — the UAE's first international hospital affiliated with a UK teaching hospital — is situated within the masterplan, providing residents with specialist medical care of the highest standard. Genesis Healthcare Centre complements the offering with primary and urgent care services.

Who Should Invest Here

Dubai Hills Estate is ideally positioned for investors seeking a balance between capital appreciation and reliable rental income within a Tier-1 masterplan community. Family offices and HNW individuals allocating AED 3M and above will find the villa market offers genuine scarcity value, strong annual rental income, and a tenant profile that minimises management friction. Mid-market investors entering at AED 1.2–2.5M via the apartment sub-market benefit from higher yield percentages and shorter void periods, with the added optionality of a well-developed resale market at handover.

Buy-to-let strategies targeting long-term family tenants, particularly expatriates relocating for children's schooling, are exceptionally well-suited to Dubai Hills Estate. The concentration of GEMS schools within the community creates lease stickiness — families who enrol children rarely move mid-tenancy. Off-plan investment at launch pricing with a view to selling at handover or retaining for three-to-five years has also generated strong risk-adjusted returns, supported by Emaar's consistent track record of delivering on schedule and sustaining post-handover capital values.

Investors with a broader Dubai portfolio should also explore Downtown Dubai for ultra-prime urban assets, and Arabian Ranches for comparable villa community investment at a different price point and risk profile.
