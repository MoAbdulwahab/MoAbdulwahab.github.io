Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Mohamed Abdulwahab, a licensed Dubai real estate broker providing off-plan and investment property advisory services ("we", "us", or "our"). Our registered address is Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: mr.mohamed.sulaiman@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it, primarily through:

  • Contact and enquiry forms — name, email address, phone number, and the nature of your enquiry.
  • Investment Calculator tool — name, email, phone, and the financial parameters you enter when requesting your results.
  • Cookies and analytics — browser type, pages visited, time on site, and approximate location. This data is collected in aggregate and does not personally identify you unless you have consented.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the advisory service you requested.
  • Send you property opportunities, market updates, and research relevant to your stated investment interests — only where you have provided consent or where we have a legitimate interest to do so.
  • Improve the content and functionality of this website.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we process your data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent — where you have provided it (e.g. accepting cookies, opting into communications).
  • Legitimate interests — to follow up on genuine property investment enquiries.
  • Contract performance — where processing is necessary to provide a service you have requested.

This website is also operated in compliance with the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) 2021.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. On your first visit, a consent banner will appear. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies. Essential cookies (which keep the site functioning) are set regardless of your choice.

Your cookie preference is stored locally in your browser under the key ma_cookie_consent. We do not share this data.

6. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Netlify — web hosting and form processing (netlify.com). Form submissions are stored on Netlify's servers and forwarded to us by email.
  • Google Fonts — typography assets loaded from Google's CDN.
  • Calendly — booking system for consultation calls (calendly.com).

7. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and form submission data for up to 3 years from the date of receipt, or longer if required by applicable law or ongoing business relationship. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 9).

8. International Transfers

As this website is hosted on Netlify (US-based infrastructure), data submitted via forms may be processed outside the UAE or EEA. Netlify maintains appropriate data transfer safeguards. By using our forms, you consent to this transfer.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the legal basis).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at mr.mohamed.sulaiman@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security

This website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted securely to Netlify. Whilst we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no internet transmission can be guaranteed as 100% secure.
