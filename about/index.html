Started with $1,000.
Now advising private investors.

I arrived in Dubai with $1,000, no place to stay and no plan. Plenty of people told me I wouldn't last in real estate, especially on a commission-only basis. I chose the role anyway.

Growing up across Kuwait, Sudan and Dubai gave me something most advisors lack: the ability to move between cultures, read people, and build trust across borders. Those aren't soft skills. They're the foundation of every acquisition I close and every investor relationship I maintain.

Today, I advise private investors and HNW individuals on off-plan acquisitions across Dubai's most sought-after developments. From investment thesis and unit-level due diligence to Golden Visa structuring and full asset management. My network spans tier-one developers, institutional investors, and a growing base of repeat clients who return because the returns speak for themselves.

As a licensed broker in Dubai, I operate with full regulatory authority. But I'm not building a traditional brokerage. I'm building a private advisory practice focused on long-term wealth creation, portfolio diversification, and becoming the definitive authority in Dubai real estate investment.
